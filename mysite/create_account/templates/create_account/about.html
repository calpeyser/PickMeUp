Welcome to PickMeUp!

Come take our tour!

First, you'll have to log in with your NetID to get into the site. PickMeUp was made just for Princeton students!

Then, let us know a little bit about yourself!

Don't worry, we'll never call you, and only users you message or ride with will ever see your phone number!

You'll arrive at our home page. You can create and search rides here, as well as navigate throughout the site using the bar at the top.

Let's say I'm planning to drive from Princeton to New York. We'll just enter the origin and destination and press 'I'm Driving.'

We'll need a bit more info, so that people will be able to find a ride that works for them!

Great! We've created our first ride.

We've been taken straight to our profile page. You can access this page at any time by pressing 'Profile' on the bar at the top.

Oh look! Your profile shows a notification when someone wants to join your ride. Let's click for more detail.

As a driver, you get to approve everyone who gets into your car.

If you'd like to talk it over first, you can send them a message!

We'll accept Valya, and then click 'Message the approved passengers in this ride'

Give your message a subject and body, and press send!

In your inbox, you can see all of your conversations.

Click on any to see the entire conversation in detail

Also, by clicking 'Write Message' at the top, you can directly message anyone who's used the site.

Now let's try searching for a ride. Let's go from Princeton to New Haven.

From our homepage, simply list the origin and destination, and press "I'm looking for a ride.

Aha! There's a ride going to Needham Heights, MA which should do nicely.

Note that PickMeUp finds any rides along which dropping you off would be convenient.

Clicking any ride will show you more details.

From here we can request to join the ride!

Congratulations! You're ready to start using the site.

May your traveling be safe, easy, and traffic-free!
